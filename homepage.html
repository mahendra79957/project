Shiva nagaraju is a Film Director And His Full Name Is Kamepalli Shiva Nagaraju. He Hail from Pedakodamagundla of Palnadu District. He was born on Aug 20,1994. He was Awarded As a Start Best Director Of The Year 2k22 with the Independent film(Who is Ramsundhar).
